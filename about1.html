Silver Medal Award

Smart Address Completion System

Type: National competitions

Description: Project competition

Date: 28 September, 2024

The project was awarded the Silver Medal at the Data Science Project Contest 2024, held at the Faculty of Science, Chiang Mai University, on September 28, 2024. It was selected as a finalist from a total of 287 teams. The competition format involved analyzing a dataset provided by the organizing committee and further developing it into a project aimed at solving real-world problems.
