Second Runner-Up Award

ICT Challenge 2024

Type: National competitions

Description: Academic Competition: Computer Science and Information Technology Quiz Contest

Date: 11 November, 2024

Im was awarded Second Runner-Up and a scholarship of 3,000 Baht at the *Computer Science and Information Technology Quiz Contest*, held at the Faculty of Information Technology, King Mongkut's University of Technology Thonburi, on November 11, 2024. The team advanced to the semi-finals as one of the top 90 teams from approximately 220 teams nationwide and ultimately secured a place in the final round, competing among the top 10 teams. The competition covered four main topics:

  • Network and Cloud Computing
  • Hardware and Operating system
  • Data Structure and Algorithm
  • Artificial Intelligence
